# Design System — Premium Portfolio & Redesign Proposal Site

## Product Context

An ultra-premium personal portfolio for a Full Stack Developer. The goal is NOT a
traditional developer portfolio — it is an agency-grade website-redesign sales
pitch. A visiting company decision-maker (CEO/exec) should immediately feel:
"This person already understands our business and knows how to improve our
website." The site blends: personal portfolio + agency credibility + redesign
proposal + business case study.

Visual reference feel: Apple + Linear + Vercel + Stripe + Framer.
Trust, expertise, innovation, premium execution. No template feeling, no
generic-portfolio look.

## Theme: Dark Luxury

- Background: `#050505`
- Card surface: `#101010`
- Primary text: `#FFFFFF`
- Secondary text: `#B0B0B0`
- Accent: `#00E5A8`
- Border: `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`

## Typography

- Fonts: Geist, Inter, Satoshi
- Huge, oversized display typography for headlines
- Clean, generous spacing
- Minimalistic layout — restraint over decoration

## Motion / Animation

- Framer Motion throughout
- Smooth scroll-triggered reveal animations
- Hover interactions on cards/buttons
- Subtle parallax
- Image zoom effects on project screenshots
- Micro-interactions everywhere — everything should feel premium and intentional
- Hero: premium laptop mockup with subtle floating animation

## Page Sections (in order)

1. **Hero** — Statement headline "I build modern digital experiences that help
   businesses grow." Subheadline about full-stack expertise, performance, UX,
   scalability. CTAs: "View My Work" / "Discuss Your Project". Premium laptop
   mockup with floating animation.
2. **About** — Business-value framed, not personal bio. Highlights: UX,
   Performance, SEO, Scalability, Maintainability, Modern Design.
3. **Featured Projects** — Premium cards, large screenshots, modern layout.
   Each: overview, problem, solution, technologies, impact. Elegant hover
   effects.
4. **What I Can Do For Your Business** — Service cards: Website Redesign,
   Corporate Website, Custom Web Application, Performance Optimization,
   UI/UX Design, Maintenance. Each explains business benefit.
5. **Website Audit** — "What I would improve on your current website." Modern
   audit-dashboard UI. Categories: Loading Speed, Mobile Experience, Visual
   Identity, Navigation, Conversion Optimization, SEO, Accessibility, Trust
   Signals. Each shows: current status indicator, improvement opportunity,
   expected impact. Should read like a real consultancy report.
6. **Before / After Transformation** — Side-by-side: Current Website vs
   Proposed Redesign. Device mockups. Highlights: modern layout, improved UX,
   better mobile design, stronger branding, faster navigation, higher
   conversion potential. Visually impressive.
7. **Redesign Vision** — "The Future Version of Your Website." Concept preview
   mockup cards: Homepage Concept, Service Pages, Mobile Version, Contact
   Experience, Modern Dashboard Concept.
8. **Development Process** — Elegant timeline, 8 steps: Discovery, Audit,
   Wireframing, UI Design, Development, Testing, Launch, Maintenance.
9. **Technology Stack** — Premium badges (not a logo wall): Next.js, React,
   TypeScript, Node.js, Laravel, PostgreSQL, TailwindCSS, Docker, Git,
   Framer Motion.
10. **Why Work With Me** — Comparison table: Typical Website vs My Approach,
    across Performance, Design Quality, Maintainability, SEO, Scalability,
    UX, Business Results.
11. **Testimonials** — Professional, clean, luxury cards.
12. **Final CTA** — Huge closing section. Headline "Ready to transform your
    online presence?" Subheadline about reflecting business quality. Buttons:
    Contact / Schedule Meeting. Premium background effects.

## Responsiveness

Pixel-perfect desktop, excellent tablet, exceptional mobile.

## Hard Constraints

Use ONLY the fonts, colors, spacing, and component styles defined above. Do
not introduce any fonts, colors, or visual styles outside this design system.
